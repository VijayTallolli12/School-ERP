# School Admin User Guide

Version: 1.0.0

Revision date: 2026-07-08

## Login

1. Open the login page and enter your email and password.
2. If the system is configured for multiple schools, choose the appropriate school context.
3. Submit the form.

## Dashboard

The dashboard shows role-based summary widgets and quick links. Administrators can access academic, finance, communication, and administration areas from the sidebar.

## Sidebar

Common navigation items include:

- Dashboard
- Students
- Teachers
- Attendance
- Exams
- Homework
- Fees
- Payroll
- Library
- Transport
- Notifications
- Settings
- Users

## Daily Tasks

- Review attendance and fee summaries.
- Create or update student and teacher profiles.
- Manage school configurations and users.
- Review notifications and AI assistant responses.

## Attendance

Attendance can be entered and reviewed from the attendance module. Reports are available for current and historical periods.

## Homework

Create homework assignments for class and subject combinations.

## Reports

Use the reports area to review student, fee, attendance, and exam reports.

## Notifications

Send and review notifications for relevant groups.

## AI

Use the Ask ERP or Executive Copilot features for operational summaries and guided workflows.

## Profile and Settings

Update user profile details and settings from the available profile and settings views.

## Logout

Use the logout action from the account menu.

## Common Errors

- Permission denied: confirm the user has the correct role and permission.
- School context missing: ensure the school selection or session is correct.

## FAQ

- Can I manage multiple schools? The system is designed for multi-school operation, but access is controlled by the active school context.
- Can teachers view reports? Only if the relevant permissions are granted.

## Best Practices

- Keep school settings current.
- Verify data before publishing results or processing payroll.
- Review AI-generated actions carefully before execution.

Version: 1.0.0

## Login

Sign in with your principal account and access the school dashboard.

## Dashboard

The principal dashboard provides oversight of attendance, timetable, exams, students, teachers, homework, fees, and leave approvals.

## Sidebar

The principal navigation includes major modules for operations and reporting.

## Daily Tasks

- Review academic and attendance trends.
- Approve or review leave requests.
- Monitor student, teacher, and examination activity.
- Use AI views for summaries.

## Attendance

Use attendance tools to review school-level attendance data.

## Homework

Review homework activity and supporting data.

## Reports

Use reports for attendance, fees, and other operational views.

## Notifications

Receive and review communication items.

## AI

The AI assistant can summarize school operations and support executive-style workflows.

## Profile and Settings

Review profile information and account settings.

## Logout

Use logout from the account menu.

## Common Errors

- 403 or missing permissions: verify role assignment.
- Missing school context: confirm the active school.

## FAQ

- Can I see teacher attendance? Yes, if the relevant permissions are present.

## Best Practices

- Review trends regularly.
- Keep approvers informed of outstanding requests.
